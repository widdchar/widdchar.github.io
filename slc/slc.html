Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the Salt Lake County Chamber of Commerce is to establish a voluntary partnership of business and professionals working together to build a healthy local economy and improve the quality of life in Salt Lake County. Our main goals this year is to 1) increase our membership by 10%, 2) promote local activities and events, and 3) encourage networking amongst Chamber of Commerce members.
