Site Name

Weather Rainbow Report

The Site's Purpose

The purpose of Weather Rainbow Report is to provide accurate weather predictions for the Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and Preston areas. It is an upbeat website that will display photos of the different areas. Residents will be able to submit and share their own photos of the weather. It will also be a place to highlight positive and uplifing news articles of events that happen in the area as well.
